Rin Park

Artist

I’ve always known that I liked drawing, but as a child I actually wanted to be a violinist. And then a composer. And then, I settled on art again simply because my highschool of choice at the time didn’t have the music class I wanted. So I decided to invest my energy back into my art again.

My personal life has, generally, always been a huge inspiration for my art, as I believe most artists would find. But regardless, I believe my art has an unique message that sets it apart from others. Funnily enough, I never set out to have a specific idea behind each art piece. Maybe it’s just how my mind works, but I like to think about it after it’s complete and see what it means.

I am, unfortunately, yet to have professional experience in this field. As of now, my art has generally been for myself.
